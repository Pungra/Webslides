What makes Pungra unique?

There are many people providing services which they are calling bhangra fitness, bhangra workout or bhangra exercise. There are a few problems.

First, those people have no exercise qualifications. Meaning they are dancers having a shot [in the dark] about what makes a good exercise class. Second, most dancers' classes are dance classes that are a bit "exercisey".

Ravi Sandhu's experience has taught him that most people want exercise classes, above dance classes.

Pungra is leading a revolution of this concept. Our format ensures that all instructors are qualified to exercise to music level 2, and our classes and videos are exercise [first] and a bit "dancey" [second].

Why does it mattter?

We live in a time where as much as 70% in the developed world are overweight. And it's anticipated by the UK NHS that 50% of the UK will be obese by 2030. We believe in serving customers well. If customer want and need exercise, that's what we should provide utmost. We should ensure Punjabi music is used legally. We should have the appropriate levels of insurance suitable for group exercise to music.
